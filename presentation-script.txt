--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  THE PROBLEM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Every day, millions of people watch the market go up and wish they had
invested. But the barrier is real: you can read all the books you want,
watch all the YouTube videos — until you actually sit down and place a
trade, you don't learn anything.

Paper trading apps exist, but they're either too complicated, too boring,
or they use fake prices that don't reflect reality. People quit in a week.

There's no consequence-free environment that actually feels real.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  THE SOLUTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

MarketSim Pro is a fully interactive stock market simulator that gives you
$100,000 in virtual cash and lets you trade with REAL market data — no
risk, no credit card, no real money ever changes hands.

[open the app — show the Dashboard]

This is the Dashboard. The moment you land here, you can see your total
portfolio value, your return, and the biggest movers in the market today —
all pulling from live Alpaca API data, refreshed in real time.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  FEATURE WALKTHROUGH
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

-- MARKETS --

[click Markets in the sidebar]

Here's every asset available to trade. We're talking 18 instruments:
major stocks like Apple, NVIDIA, Tesla, Microsoft, Amazon — ETFs like
SPY and QQQ — and even Bitcoin. Every single one with a live price and
today's change percentage.

You can filter by sector — Technology, Financials, Healthcare, Energy,
Crypto — or search by name. Hover over any row and you get a tooltip
showing open, high, low, previous close, and volume. Click any stock and
you go straight to its chart and trade panel.

-- STOCK DETAIL PAGE --

[click AAPL]

Every stock has a full candlestick chart powered by TradingView's
lightweight charts library. You can see the price history, hover for
OHLC data on any candle, and scroll left to load more history
automatically.

Each stock is labeled with a risk level — Low, Medium, High, or Extreme
for crypto. You can buy and sell directly from this page. Enter shares,
see the total cost update live, hit Buy — it instantly updates your
portfolio and cash balance.

You can also star any stock from here to add it to your Watchlist.

-- PORTFOLIO --

[click Portfolio]

Your Portfolio page tracks every open position in real time — shares held,
average cost, current price, unrealized P&L per holding. At the top you
see total portfolio value, total cash remaining, and your overall return.

It also breaks down your sector allocation so you can see if you're
over-concentrated. If more than 40% of your portfolio is in one single
stock, it warns you about diversification risk — just like a real
advisor would.

You can reset your portfolio at any time and start fresh with $100,000.

-- SIMULATION MODE --

[click Simulation]

This is the centerpiece of MarketSim Pro — and honestly, what makes it
unlike anything else out there.

Simulation Mode lets you pick any stock and a date range, and then watch
the market unfold bar by bar — like rewinding time and trading through it
live.

But here's where it gets interesting.

The moment you hit Start, our AI — powered by Claude via OpenRouter —
analyses that stock and makes a prediction: Bullish, Bearish, or Neutral,
with a confidence score and a written explanation of exactly why.

[point to the AI Reasoning card]

Right here — the "Why Bullish?" card — the AI tells you the three key
factors driving its call, weighted by impact. High confidence bullish
prediction? The candlestick chart will trend upward. Bearish call? You'll
watch it fall. The chart is literally driven by the AI's prediction.

[point to the chart]

Each bar is generated using the AI's direction and confidence as the
actual drift — not random noise. If the AI says 72% bullish, the chart
reflects that. You can trade through it in real time: buy at the dip,
sell at the peak, or hold through volatility.

You can run it at 1x speed, 10x, even 100x — simulating an entire year
of trading in seconds.

-- WATCHLIST --

[click Watchlist]

Any stock you've starred from its detail page shows up here. Live prices,
updated constantly. It's your personal shortlist — stocks you're tracking
or planning to trade next.

-- NEWS & SENTIMENT --

[click News]

The News feed shows real market headlines — earnings results, Fed
decisions, sector moves. Every article has a sentiment label: green for
bullish signals, red for bearish, grey for neutral. Filter by category —
Earnings, Macro, Crypto, Energy — to focus on what matters to your
positions.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  THE FIRST-TIME EXPERIENCE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

New users get a guided tutorial that auto-launches on first visit. It
walks you through every section of the app — what each page does, what
to click, tips for getting started — all without leaving the app. And if
you ever need it again, one click at the bottom of the sidebar brings it
back.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  THE TECH STACK
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Built with Next.js 15 and TypeScript. Live price data from the Alpaca
Markets API. Candlestick charts from TradingView's Lightweight Charts
library. AI predictions powered by Claude Haiku via OpenRouter. Everything
runs in the browser — no backend database, no user accounts, no personal
data stored anywhere.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  WHO IT'S FOR
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Students who want to learn investing before they have real money to risk.
Beginners who are curious about the market but scared to start.
Anyone who wants to test a strategy — sector rotation, buy-the-dip,
momentum trading — without putting a single dollar on the line.

The total addressable market? Roughly 100 million people in the US alone
who are interested in investing but haven't started yet.
